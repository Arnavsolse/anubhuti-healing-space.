🧠 Therapy Types

The Right Support for Every Need

We offer a range of therapy formats, each tailored to different life situations and healing goals.

🧍

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions focused on anxiety, depression, trauma, self-esteem, life transitions, and personal growth. Fully confidential and completely client-led.

💑

Couples Counselling

Safe space for partners to improve communication, work through conflict, process infidelity, or decide the future of their relationship with professional guidance.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Therapy

Address dynamics, conflict, parenting challenges, and transitions as a family system — creating healthier patterns that benefit every member at home.

🧒

Child & Adolescent

Play therapy, expressive arts, and age-appropriate counselling for children and teens dealing with anxiety, school stress, relationships, and identity.

🌐

Online Therapy

Same quality care, from the comfort of your home. Secure video sessions available for all therapy types — ideal for busy schedules or those outside Pune.

🎨

Expressive Art Therapy

Healing through art, music, movement, and puppetry — particularly powerful for those who find words limiting or are processing deep emotional experiences.

👥 Who We Help

You Don't Need to Be in Crisis to Seek Help

Therapy is for anyone who wants to understand themselves better, navigate life more smoothly, or heal from past pain.

😟
Anxiety & Stress

Persistent worry, panic attacks, social anxiety, performance anxiety, or burnout.

💔
Grief & Loss

Bereavement, relationship endings, job loss, or any form of significant change or loss.

🌑
Depression

Low mood, lack of motivation, hopelessness, or emptiness — mild to severe.

🧩
Trauma & PTSD

Past experiences — childhood, relationships, accidents — that continue to affect daily life.

🔄
Life Transitions

Career changes, moving cities, becoming a parent, marriage, divorce, retirement.

🌿
Personal Growth

Better self-awareness, healthier relationships, or simply living a more intentional life.
