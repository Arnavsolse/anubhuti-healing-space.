🌟 Programme Benefits

What You Gain from Interning with Us

Our internship is built to give psychology students the most comprehensive real-world training possible.

👩‍⚕️

Direct Client Work

Work with real clients under close supervision — individual, group, and family cases across diverse presentations.

📝

Certified Completion

Receive a formal certificate of completion, valid for university submissions and recognised by professional bodies.

🧑‍🏫

1:1 Supervision

Weekly individual supervision with a senior clinician to reflect on cases, skill gaps, and personal development.

🎨

Multi-Modal Training

Gain exposure to art therapy, music, play therapy, group facilitation, and EMDR observation — beyond textbook learning.

📚

Weekly Case Discussions

Group case presentation sessions for a safe space to discuss client scenarios, ethical dilemmas, and evidence-based interventions.

🌐

Flexible Schedule

Part-time and full-time batches available, both in-person and hybrid — designed to work around your academic calendar.

✅ Eligibility

Who Can Apply?

  • Currently enrolled in a BSc / BA / MSc / MA Psychology programme
  • Internships required by your university syllabus are welcome
  • Genuine interest in clinical or counselling psychology
  • Ability to commit 3–6 months to the programme
  • Willingness to attend weekly group supervision sessions
  • Basic proficiency in English and Marathi / Hindi
📅 Application Process

How to Apply

1

Submit Your Application

Fill our internship form with your academic background and areas of interest.

2

Orientation Interview

A 30-minute call with our clinical lead to assess fit and discuss your learning goals.

3

Placement & Induction

Begin with a structured 2-week induction — ethics, client confidentiality, and observation.

4

Active Clinical Work

Co-facilitate sessions, maintain case notes, and present in weekly supervision groups.

5

Certification

Submit final reflective report and receive your certificate at the end of your internship.

Ready to Launch Your Psychology Career?

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. Reach out to check current batch availability.
