✨ Why Join a Workshop

What Makes Our Workshops Different

Beyond technique — our workshops are designed to create genuine connection and lasting personal insights.

🎭
No Skills Required

Every workshop is designed for complete beginners. The process matters, not the product.

👩‍⚕️
Therapist-Facilitated

All sessions are led by trained mental health professionals, not just instructors.

🔒
Safe & Confidential

Group agreements ensure a safe, judgment-free container for every participant.

🌱
Community Building

Beyond healing yourself, you build a tribe of like-minded people on similar journeys.

💰
Affordable Access

Workshops are priced to be significantly more accessible than individual therapy sessions.

📍
In-Person & Online

Most workshops are offered both in-person at our Erandwane space and via live video.
